FAQ

How do I import my word list?
Export a CSV and import it inside the app (CSV format depends on your in-app import screen).

How do I sync between devices?
Enable iCloud for Vocatbulary in iOS Settings.

Does the app collect data?
No. The app does not collect personal data. See the Privacy Policy.
