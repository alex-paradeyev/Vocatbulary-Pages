Vocatbulary – Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-17

Summary: Vocatbulary does not collect personal data from users. Learning progress is stored on the device and can be synced via iCloud if the user enables iCloud.

1) Who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to the iOS app Vocatbulary (“the App”).

Contact: support@paradeyev.com

2) Data the App collects

We do not collect personal data from the App (no analytics tracking, no advertising identifiers, no third-party trackers).

3) Data stored on your device

The App stores learning data locally on your device, for example:

  • selected dictionaries and word lists (including imported CSV lists),
  • answers (right/wrong), spaced repetition schedule,
  • progress metrics (accuracy, streaks, XP/level),
  • app preferences (languages, reminders settings).

4) iCloud sync

If you have iCloud enabled, the App can sync your learning progress across your devices using Apple iCloud/CloudKit. Apple may process this data as part of providing iCloud services.

You can disable iCloud for the App in iOS Settings to stop syncing.

5) Notifications (Reminders)

If you enable reminders, the App may send local notifications on your device. No notification data is sent to us.

6) Children’s privacy

The App is designed for children and bilingual families. We do not collect personal information from children. There is no user-generated content and no in-app advertising.

7) Third-party services

The App uses Apple system services only (e.g., iCloud if enabled). The App does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

8) Your choices

  • You can delete the App to remove locally stored data.
  • You can disable iCloud syncing for the App in iOS Settings.
  • You can disable notifications in iOS Settings.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will be available at this page.

10) Contact

If you have questions, contact us at support@paradeyev.com.
